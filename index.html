From Knitter to Designer

I love knitting so much, I actually became a designer - how about that?! The photo above is from a magazine shoot from two years ago, when my original design was including in a national publication for knitters. So, how do you do it?

Start Somewhere

The first step to becoming a knitwear designer is passion. After that, you just have to start somewhere. Here are different ways to gather inspiration:

  • Visit your local yarn store and pick out some of your favorite colors.
  • Flip through some magazines and see what's trending in the fashion world.
  • Draw out some sketches of knitwear.
  • Look through a stitch dictionary.
  • Just cast-on some yarn and knit away
